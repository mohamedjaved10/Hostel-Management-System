About The Hostel

This portal is designed to improve hostel administration, complaint handling, maintenance monitoring, communication and student support services through a centralized digital platform.

Hostel Overview

The hostel provides accommodation, food services, study facilities, recreational facilities and student support services. The objective of this portal is to improve transparency and service management.

Hostel Facilities

  • Accommodation Facilities
  • Mess & Dining Services
  • Drinking Water Facilities
  • WiFi Connectivity
  • Sports Room
  • Playground Facilities
  • Study & Reading Areas
  • Security Services
  • Maintenance Support
  • Emergency Assistance

Hostel Rules & Regulations

  • Maintain discipline inside hostel premises.
  • Respect fellow students and staff members.
  • Avoid ragging, harassment and misconduct.
  • Follow hostel timings and regulations.
  • Keep rooms and common areas clean.
  • Report damages and maintenance issues promptly.
  • Use hostel facilities responsibly.

Complaint Resolution Workflow

  1. Student submits complaint.
  2. Complaint is reviewed by administration.
  3. Complaint is assigned to responsible staff.
  4. Issue is resolved and updated.
  5. Complaint is marked as completed.

3-Day Resolution Commitment

The hostel administration aims to review and process complaints within three working days wherever applicable. Emergency complaints are prioritized immediately.

Permission Request Policy

  • Late entry requests should be submitted in advance.
  • Emergency leave requests require proper justification.
  • Return dates and timings must be provided accurately.
  • Administration approval is required before departure.

Emergency Reporting Process

Emergency complaints such as ragging, theft, harassment and safety threats are given immediate attention and administrative review.

Student Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate complaint information.
  • Follow hostel rules and guidelines.
  • Maintain cleanliness and discipline.
  • Use facilities responsibly.
  • Report emergencies immediately.

Administration Responsibilities

  • Review complaints promptly.
  • Monitor hostel facilities.
  • Manage notices and announcements.
  • Process permission requests.
  • Ensure student safety and welfare.
